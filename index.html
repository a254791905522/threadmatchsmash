Unchain the Beasts

Untangle · Match · Solve in 3D

Free 3D Puzzle iPad 4+

Untangle monster chains, match pairs, and solve tricky 3D spatial puzzles before time runs out. Orbit and zoom the camera around a 3D web of creatures linked by chains. Find monster pairs with a clear, unblocked line of sight—tap them and they walk together, merge into a larger beast, and jump to the platform. If their path is blocked, the match fails. Clear every chain on the board before the timer or move limit runs out. Earn coins to spend on power-ups: extra time, extra moves, hints, or skip tough levels.

Why play

  • 3D spatial gameplay – Rotate and zoom to see the full puzzle.
  • Line-of-sight matching – Only unblocked pairs can merge.
  • Chain-clearing goal – Free all beasts from the web.
  • Time & move limits – Plan your taps carefully.
  • Coins & power-ups – Hints, extra time, extra moves, skip.
  • Designed for iPad – Big screen, smooth controls.
